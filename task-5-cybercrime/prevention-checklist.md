# Phishing Prevention Checklist

## Safety Measures to Prevent Phishing Attacks

- Do not click on suspicious links received through email or messages.
- Always check the sender's email address carefully.
- Verify website URLs before entering login details.
- Use strong and unique passwords for different accounts.
- Enable two-factor authentication (2FA) for better security.
- Avoid sharing personal or banking details through email.
- Install antivirus software and keep it updated.
- Be cautious of urgent messages asking for immediate action.
- Regularly monitor bank statements for unusual activity.
- Report phishing attempts to official cybercrime authorities.

## Reporting Cybercrime
