# Task 1 — Digital Literacy Awareness Infographic

## Objective

The objective of this task was to create a digital literacy awareness infographic that highlights the importance of safe internet usage and responsible online behavior.

---

## Tool Used

Canva

---

## Description

In this task, an infographic was created using Canva to spread awareness about digital literacy. The infographic includes key topics such as:

- Definition of Digital Literacy  
- Safe Internet Practices  
- Professional Online Presence  
- Email Etiquette  

Visual elements such as icons, colors, and headings were used to make the information clear and visually appealing.

---

## Outcome

This task helped improve my ability to present information visually and understand the importance of digital literacy in everyday life.
