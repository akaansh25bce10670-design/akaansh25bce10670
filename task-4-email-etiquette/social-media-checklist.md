# Social Media Etiquette Checklist

## Responsible Social Media Usage Guidelines

- Use strong and secure passwords for all social media accounts.
- Enable two-factor authentication whenever possible.
- Do not share personal or confidential information publicly.
- Always verify information before sharing it online.
- Maintain respectful communication and avoid offensive language.
- Avoid posting inappropriate or harmful content.
- Respect privacy of others and do not share others' information without permission.
- Be cautious while accepting friend or follow requests from unknown people.
- Regularly review privacy settings of social media accounts.
- Report suspicious or abusive content immediately.
