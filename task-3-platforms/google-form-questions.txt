Google Form Title:
Digital Literacy Awareness Survey

Description:
This survey aims to understand students' awareness about digital literacy, cybersecurity, and safe internet practices.

Questions:

1. What does digital literacy mean?
Options:
- Ability to use digital devices and internet
- Ability to play games
- Ability to use social media only
- None of the above

Correct Answer: Ability to use digital devices and internet

--------------------------------------------------

2. Which of the following is a safe internet practice?
Options:
- Sharing passwords with friends
- Clicking unknown links
- Using strong passwords
- Ignoring security updates

Correct Answer: Using strong passwords

--------------------------------------------------

3. What should you do if you receive a suspicious email?
Options:
- Open all links immediately
- Share personal information
- Ignore and delete the email
- Forward it to unknown people

Correct Answer: Ignore and delete the email

--------------------------------------------------
